# Code of Conduct

## 1. Project Ownership & Rules

This project is authored and maintained by **Raihan Marwanda ([@sijawiir](https://github.com/sijawiir))**.

- **Usage and Forking**: You are encouraged to fork and modify this project for your own needs.
- **Attribution**: You may not claim this project as your own. Proper attribution to the original author is required if this project is used in publications or derivative works.
- **Contributions**: Pull Requests are welcome, provided they adhere to the project's coding standards.

## 2. Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone. We expect contributors to:

- **Demonstrate Empathy**: Be kind and respectful toward other people.
- **Be Professional**: Focus on what is best for the community and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- **Prohibited Behavior**: Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks, and any form of public or private harassment are strictly forbidden.

## 3. Enforcement

The maintainer, **@sijawiir**, is responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. We reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## 4. Reporting & Consequences

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported directly to:

- **Maintainer**: Raihan Marwanda
- **GitHub**: [@sijawiir](https://github.com/sijawiir)

Violations may result in a private warning, temporary interaction restrictions, or a permanent ban from the repository.
